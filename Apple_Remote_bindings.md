
# Apple Remote quick guide for normal humans

In Menus
    - "Menu" generally = "back to the previous menu"
    - long press "Menu" to show the power menu, click exit to quit Kodi (saves power)
    - long press the middle button to show context menus (e.g. to mark a show/episode as watched)
    
While Playing
    - middle button opens the OSD controls
    - Up opens the subtitle delay control (press up/down to close it)
    - long press Up to open the subtitles menu (e.g. to download, change language)
    - Down opens the audio delay control (press up/down to close it)
    - long press Down to open the audio menu (e.g. to change language)
